Richard E. Grant

Richard E. Grant (born Richard Grant Esterhuysen;, 5 May 1957) is a British actor, screenwriter, and director. He came to public attention in 1987 for playing Withnail in the film Withnail and I, and achieved international recognition as John Seward in the 1992 blockbuster Bram Stoker's Dracula.
